Museum Rules

  • Respect artifacts and exhibits.
  • No flash photography.
  • Do not touch artifacts unless permitted.
  • Keep noise to a minimum.
  • Follow guided tour instructions.
  • No food or drinks in exhibit areas.
  • Observe dress code if specified.
  • Children must be accompanied by adults.
  • No smoking inside the museum.
  • Observe all posted signs and warnings.
  • Mobile phones must be on silent mode.
  • Do not disturb other visitors.
  • Respect the cultural sensitivities of others.
  • No pets allowed, except for service animals.
  • Do not litter; use designated bins for trash.
  • Do not deface or vandalize museum property.
  • Report any damage or suspicious behavior to staff.
  • Respect queue lines and wait your turn.
  • Photography may be restricted in certain areas.
  • No running or rough play inside the museum.
  • Only pencils allowed for note-taking, no pens.

Thank you for helping us preserve and protect our cultural heritage!
