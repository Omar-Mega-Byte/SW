Facilities Information

Here the Facilities page you can Access the Library, Archives, Cafes and study rooms

Library

The library offers a wide range of books, journals, and multimedia resources. It also provides study spaces and computer facilities.

Archives

The archives contain historical documents, manuscripts, and records. Visitors can access materials related to various subjects and periods.

Cafes

Our cafes serve a variety of beverages, snacks, and light meals. Enjoy a cozy atmosphere while you relax.

Study Rooms

Our Study rooms serve a Great, Calm and Elegant atmosphere. Enjoy a Fantastic place and free Wi-Fi while you study.

Accessibility Information

Provide accessibility information for each facility. Here's how to experience or use them:

  • Library: Wheelchair ramps and elevators are available for easy access to all floors.
  • Archives: Staff members are available to assist visitors with disabilities in accessing materials.
  • Cafes: Menus are available in braille upon request.
